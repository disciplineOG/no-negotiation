Daily Routine
6:30 AM — Wakeup
Wake up. Drink 500–800ml warm water immediately. Do not eat — fasted state maximises fat burn.
6:30–7:10 AM — Morning Cardio
Cycling / jogging / brisk walking outdoors 30–40 min. Fasted cardio = direct fat burn. Target heart rate 120–140 BPM.
7:15–9:30 AM — Freshen up + Meal 1 Prep
Shower. Steam/boil veggies. Blend dressing. Assemble salad box. Pack lunch box + dressing jar separately.
10:00 AM — Commute to Office
Carry Meal 1 box + dressing jar + water bottle. Continue sipping water during commute.
10:00–1:30 PM — Work
No snacking — extend fasted window for maximum fat loss. Sip water.
1:30–2:30 PM — Lunch (Meal 1)
Start with lemon water. Then eat full salad bowl — pour dressing when eating. Heat veggies in office oven if available. Eat slowly.
2:30–3:30 PM — Work
Sip water. Post-meal digestion walk if possible (5 min).
3:30–4:00 PM — Green Tea
1 cup green tea (no sugar, no milk). Boosts afternoon metabolism, reduces hunger.
4:00–4:30 PM — Work
4:30–5:00 PM — Commute Home
5:00–5:30 PM — Rest + Pre-Workout Snack
1 ripe banana (potassium, B6, fast energy) OR 5 soaked dates + 5 almonds. Rest 20–25 min before gym.
5:30–6:30 PM — Gym
Strength training — compound lifts: squats, deadlifts, bench, rows. 45–60 min. Builds muscle while in deficit.
6:30–7:00 PM — Coconut Water + Rest
200ml coconut water immediately post-gym. Replenishes electrolytes, potassium. Shower.
7:00–7:30 PM — Meal 2 Prep
Start chicken chunks pan-fry. Boil sweet potato + egg simultaneously. Takes ~20–25 min total.
8:00–8:30 PM — Dinner (Meal 2)
Eat pan-fried chicken + veggie bowl. Must finish by 8:30 PM latest (close eating window).
8:30–10:30 PM — Wind Down
Soak almonds + walnuts for tomorrow. Light stretching. Skin care. Dim lights from 10 PM. No screens 30 min before sleep.
10:30 PM — Sleep
8 hours. Growth hormone peaks during sleep — critical for fat loss and muscle repair.

Preparation Guide
Night Before (5 min)
Soak 10 almonds and 4 walnut halves in separate small bowls. Cover. Soak overnight 8 hours.
Morning Step 1 — Boil Beetroot (20 min · 800W)
Peel and dice beetroot. Add to pot with water to cover. Induction 800W. Boil 20 min. Drain. Cool. (Longest step — start first.)
Morning Step 2 — Steam all veggies (8–10 min · 500W)
Place carrot pieces in steamer basket first (needs 8 min). After 2 min add whole green beans (need 6 min). After 3 more min add broccoli florets (need 5 min). Final 2 min add capsicum. All done together in one steamer. Induction 500W throughout.
Morning Step 3 — Blend Dressing (2 min)
Peel soaked almonds (slide skin off). Add to blender: paneer 40g + curd 30g + lemon juice 10ml + 2 garlic cloves (peeled) + 1 green chilli + coriander + salt to taste. Blend smooth. Pour into small jar with lid.
Morning Step 4 — Assemble Salad Box
In large airtight container: layer steamed carrot, beans, broccoli, capsicum, boiled beetroot. Add raw tomato (seeds removed) and onion rings. Place soaked almonds + walnut halves on top. Scatter chia seeds, ground flax, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds. Seal box. Carry dressing jar SEPARATELY — pour only when eating.
Do NOT pour dressing into box before leaving home — it makes everything soggy by lunchtime. Always carry in separate jar and mix at office just before eating.

Preparation Guide (20–25 min total)
Step 1 — Marinate Chicken (before gym or on return)
Ask butcher to cut breast into curry-size chunks (~3–4cm). Mix marinade: ½ tsp salt + ½ tsp red chilli powder + ¼ tsp turmeric + 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste + juice of ½ lemon. Coat chunks well. Marinate minimum 30 min. Best: marinate morning in fridge, ready when you return from gym.
Step 2 — Boil Sweet Potato + Egg simultaneously (12 min · 800W)
Add sweet potato chunks + 1 egg to pot. Fill water to cover. Induction 800W. Start timer. Remove egg at 12 min exactly (hard boil). Continue potato 2–3 more min if needed. Cool egg in cold water, peel.
Step 3 — Pan-fry Chicken Chunks (10–12 min)
Heat pan on 300W for 1 min. Add 1 tsp coconut oil. Place marinated chunks in single layer — do not overcrowd. Set 500W. Cook 5–6 min stirring occasionally until all sides are sealed and slightly charred. Chunks cook faster than whole breast — done when no pink inside. Remove and keep aside.
Step 4 — Stir-fry Veggies in same pan (7 min · 500W)
Same pan (residual marinade flavour is the seasoning). Add onion first → cook 2 min. Add whole beans + capsicum → cook 2 min. Add broccoli last → cook 2 min. Stir constantly. Season with salt + black pepper. Veggies should have slight char, not soggy. Remove from heat.
Step 5 — Plate
Place chicken chunks + veggie stir-fry in bowl. Add boiled sweet potato alongside. Halve the egg, place on side. Add 80g cold curd in small bowl on side. Sprinkle 10g pumpkin seeds + 8g ground flax over chicken. Squeeze lemon. Eat immediately — do not reheat.
